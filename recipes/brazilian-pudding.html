Brazilian Pudding

Easy to make yet delicious dessert from Brazil

It's a 4 ingredient recipe using eggs, condensed milk, sugar and milk. Combining a creamy texture with a sweet flavour.


Ingredients:

For the Custard

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 x 14oz of sweet condensed milk

For the Caramel

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup water

Instructions:

  1. Add to a blender the eggs, milk and sweet condensed
    milk and beat for 3 minutes on medium-high speed.
    Save the mixture, it'll be used later.

  2. Add the sugar to a pan and take to the stove on medium heat.
    Stir with a wooden spoon until the sugar melt and acquire a
    golden caramel color. Put this caramel inside a round cake pan
    with a closed hole inside, and wait until it cool down for around 15 minutes.

  3. Boil water in quantity sufficient to fill a rectangular
    cake pan to the middle. Turn the oven on 482ºF (250ºC), and put the
    hot water inside the rectangular cake pan.

  4. Put the previous prepared mixture inside the circular cake pan
    with a aluminum foil protecting the mixture. Put the circular
    cake pan inside the rectangular cake pan filled with hot water
    that is inside the oven, and let it cook for around 60 minutes.

  5. Let it cool down for around 2 to 3 hours at the fridge, and it's ready
